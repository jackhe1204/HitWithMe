The Coach

These Terms and Conditions govern all tennis coaching services provided by Jack ("the Coach"), operating as an independent freelance tennis coach in the Eastern Suburbs of Sydney, NSW. By booking a session, making payment, or signing the accompanying Liability Waiver, the client ("you") agrees to be bound by these Terms in full.

Qualifications

Jack holds the following current credentials:

  • Tennis Australia Accreditation — current and valid
  • Court permit — Eastern Suburbs community tennis courts
  • Sport Science student — University of Technology Sydney (UTS)
  • 3+ years swim coaching experience
  • ATPCA insurance — currently pending; interim policy applies
Services Offered

Jack provides private and small-group tennis coaching sessions for adult clients (18+) of beginner to early-intermediate ability. Services include:

  • Trial session (1-on-1): Includes a free 15-minute discovery call. Required before accessing any other session type.
  • Trial session (2–4 people): Group trial — required before the group can book standard or package sessions.

Jack does not currently offer coaching to clients under 18. This will be reviewed once full ATPCA accreditation and insurance is obtained.

Eligibility

Medical or Illness Cancellations — Within 24 Hours
Medical Certificate Exception
If a client is unable to attend a session due to genuine illness and provides a valid medical certificate or doctor's note dated the same day as the missed session, a full session credit will be issued regardless of the notice period. This exception exists to protect clients who become unexpectedly unwell — even within the 48-hour no-refund window.

The following conditions apply to this exception:

  • Documentation required: A valid medical certificate or doctor's note must be submitted to Jack within 24 hours of the scheduled session start time. Pharmacy receipts or general "feeling unwell" messages are not sufficient — a medical certificate from a registered GP or medical centre is required.
  • Credit issued: A full session credit (make-up lesson) will be issued. This is not a cash refund — the credit may be applied to any future session within 3 months of the missed session date.
  • Session cost: The session fee is non-recoverable. Any costs already incurred by Jack will not be reimbursed in this case.
  • Frequency limit: This medical exception applies once per client per 6-month period. A second medical cancellation within the same 6-month window will be assessed at Jack's sole discretion. This limit exists to maintain fairness and prevent misuse of the policy.
  • No cash refund: Even with a valid medical certificate, the remedy is a session credit only — not a cash refund via Stripe.

Jack reserves the right to decline a medical credit claim if the documentation provided does not appear genuine or was not submitted within the required timeframe.

Repeated Short-Notice Cancellations

Where a client cancels with less than 48 hours notice on more than two occasions within any 3-month period, Jack reserves the right to require pre-payment of two sessions in advance before confirming any further bookings for that client.

Late Arrivals & No-Shows
▾
Late Arrivals

Sessions begin at the scheduled start time regardless of client arrival. If a client arrives late, the session will run for the remaining time only — the session end time will not be extended. No partial refund or credit is available for time lost due to a client's late arrival.

Jack will wait at the court for a maximum of 15 minutes past the scheduled start time before treating the session as a no-show. This wait time may be extended by mutual agreement negotiated with Jack on the day — call or text Jack on 0426 752 877 if you are running late.

No-Shows

If a client does not attend a booked session and has not provided any prior notice, the session is treated as a no-show. The full session fee will be forfeited with no refund or credit issued. Jack will send one notification to confirm the no-show before leaving the venue.

Coach Late Arrival

If Jack is running late, he will notify the client as soon as possible. If Jack is more than 15 minutes late and has not provided prior notice, the client is entitled to a full refund or a free reschedule at their choice.

Weather & Court Conditions
▾
Venue Wet Weather Policies — Waverley Park, Northbridge Park & Glebe Tennis Court

All coaching venues (Waverley Park, Northbridge Park, and Glebe Tennis Court) are outdoor facilities. If conditions are deemed unplayable by Jack, no cash refunds are issued for weather-affected sessions — a session credit (make-up lesson) is provided instead.

How you will be notified
Jack will assess conditions before every session and will call or text you on 0426 752 877 if the session is deemed unplayable. Do not assume a session is cancelled due to rain unless you have received direct notification from Jack. If you have not heard from Jack, assume the session is going ahead. This is consistent with the notification approach used by all three approved venues.
Who Decides If It Is Unplayable

Jack has sole discretion to determine whether weather or court conditions are unplayable. Relevant factors include active rainfall, standing water on the court surface, lightning within the area, extreme wind, or a court closure notice issued by the venue. A client's personal preference not to play in light drizzle does not constitute unplayable conditions — if Jack deems it playable and attends, the session is considered confirmed and no credit will be issued for client non-attendance.

Session Cancelled Before It Starts — Full Credit

If Jack determines conditions are unplayable before the session commences and notifies the client, a full session credit will be issued. This credit is redeemable as a make-up lesson at a mutually agreed future time. No cash refund will be issued. Jack will make this call and notify the client as early as possible — ideally at least 2 hours before the scheduled start time.

Session Interrupted After It Has Started

If a session has commenced but is cut short due to weather, the following applies:

  • Less than 20 minutes completed — full session credit issued (make-up lesson)
  • Between 20 and 40 minutes completed — Jack will use his sole discretion to determine whether a partial credit or a make-up reschedule is appropriate, taking into account the amount of coaching delivered and the nature of the interruption. Jack's decision is final.
  • More than 40 minutes of a 60-minute session completed — session is considered complete. No credit or reschedule will be issued.

All credits issued under this clause are for make-up coaching sessions only and are not redeemable for cash. Credits expire 3 months from the date of issue if not used.

Extreme Heat

During periods of extreme heat (above 38°C or where the Bureau of Meteorology has issued a heat health alert for Sydney), Jack reserves the right to shorten, modify or cancel sessions for the safety of both parties. The same credit policy applies as for wet weather — no cash refunds, session credits or make-up lessons offered on a pro-rated basis at Jack's discretion.

Lightning

In the event of lightning within the immediate area, the session will be suspended immediately and both parties must leave the court. This is a strict safety requirement at all approved venues. If a session is suspended due to lightning and cannot resume within the remaining booked time, the interrupted session credit policy above applies.
