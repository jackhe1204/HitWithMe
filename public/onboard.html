Terms & Conditions

Please read and scroll through the full terms before proceeding. You must reach the bottom to continue.

1. Services & Trial-First Policy

Start Tennis Sydney offers private and small-group tennis coaching for adults (18+) of beginner to early-intermediate ability. All new clients must complete a trial session before accessing standard sessions or the 4-session pack. The trial is the entry point to all coaching services.

1-on-1 trial clients receive an exclusive free 15-minute discovery call with Jack prior to their on-court session. Group trial clients (2–4 people) do not receive the discovery call.

2. Payment — Stripe Only, Always Before the Session

Payment must be completed in full via Stripe before any session time is confirmed. Clients cannot schedule their session until payment has been received. There is no pay-on-the-day, pay-after, or reserve-now-pay-later arrangement.

After payment is confirmed, Jack will personally send a confirmation page containing Cal.com scheduling links for the discovery call and on-court session. All payments are processed exclusively through Stripe. No cash, bank transfer, PayID or cheque is accepted.

3. Pricing — All-Inclusive

All session rates are fully all-inclusive. The price you see is the total you pay — no separate court hire, no hidden fees. Trial rates: Waverley Park $90 · Northbridge Park $70. Standard: Waverley $130 · Northbridge $110. 4-session pack: Waverley $110 · Northbridge $90 per session. Group rates vary by group size — see starttennissydney.com for full pricing.

4. Venues

Sessions are held at Waverley Park (Cnr Goldie Avenue & James Street, NSW 2022) or Northbridge Park & Memorial Reserve (Sailors Bay Road, Northbridge NSW 2063). Jack arranges all court bookings — clients do not need to book the court separately. Venue is confirmed in writing before payment is due.

5. Cancellation Policy

More than 48 hours notice: Full cash refund via Stripe.

Less than 48 hours notice: No refund. No exceptions unless a valid GP medical certificate is provided within 24 hours of the missed session — in which case a session credit (valid 3 months) is issued. This exception applies once per 6-month period.

No-show: Full session fee forfeited. No refund or credit issued.

Jack cancels: If Jack cancels and cannot offer a make-up session within 30 days, a full Stripe refund is issued.

6. Late Arrivals

Jack will wait up to 15 minutes for a late client. The session will not be extended. If a client has not arrived within 15 minutes without prior notice, the session may be treated as a no-show at Jack's discretion.

7. Group Size Flexibility (2–4 People)

Clients may attend with fewer people than originally booked. Price adjusts to actual attendance if 48 hours notice is given. If only 1 person attends a group booking, the 1-on-1 standard rate applies. No adjustment is made for unannounced no-shows within the group.

8. 4-Session Credit Pack

The 4-session pack is available once per new client or group only. Credits expire 6 months from purchase and are non-transferable. Standard cancellation policy applies per session credit. Credits are non-refundable except at Jack's sole discretion in exceptional circumstances.

9. Weather Cancellations

If conditions are deemed unplayable by Jack, no cash refund is issued — a session credit (make-up lesson) is provided instead. Jack will notify clients as early as possible via SMS if a session is cancelled due to weather.

10. Health & Safety

By proceeding, you confirm you are aged 18 or over, physically capable of participating in recreational tennis, and have disclosed any relevant medical conditions or injuries to Jack prior to your first session. Jack holds current ATPCA coaching insurance including Public Liability and Professional Indemnity coverage.

11. Privacy

Personal information collected (name, date of birth, contact details, tennis background) is used solely for the purpose of providing coaching services. It is not shared with third parties except where required by law. Data is stored securely and may be used to send session reminders or booking confirmations.

12. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia. By proceeding you agree to these terms in full. These terms were last updated April 2026.

— End of Terms & Conditions —
