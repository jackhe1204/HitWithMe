About Jack

Jack is the founder of Start Tennis Sydney and a Sport Science student at UTS. With 8 years of playing experience and a background in coaching, he's building something bigger than lessons — a community of passionate coaches dedicated to getting beginners to the fun part of the game, fast.

The vision is simple: skip the frustrating grind and get you into real rallies, intense hit-outs with friends, and those moments on court you actually want to talk about.

"Growing up I struggled with tennis — not from lack of drive, but because no one explained the basics clearly. That's exactly why I built this. Too many beginners never make it to the fun part. We're changing that."
